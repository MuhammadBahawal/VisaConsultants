ABOUT VISIT VISA

Quick Guide to Visit Visa Application

Application & Requirements:
Plan your visit and determine visa requirements. Prepare necessary documents including passport, travel itinerary, proof of financial support, accommodation details, and travel insurance.

Document Submission:
Submit your visa application with all required documents. Processing times vary by country and typically take 2-4 weeks. Pay the applicable visa fee.

Interview & Approval:
Some countries require a personal interview. Be prepared to discuss your travel plans, financial situation, and ties to your home country.

Visa Issuance & Travel:
Upon approval, collect your visa. Ensure your passport validity covers your entire stay. Book your flights and accommodation, and prepare for your journey.
